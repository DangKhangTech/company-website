AI Songs: Music & Melody Maker

AI Songs: Music & Melody Maker is a creative music-making app that allows you to compose, explore, and listen to original songs powered by artificial intelligence. Whether you're a music lover or an aspiring artist, this app helps you turn your ideas into melodies in seconds.

Key Features:

  • AI Music Generation: Generate unique songs and melodies instantly with the power of AI. Simply enter your idea or mood, and let the app compose music that fits your style.
  • Multiple Genres & Moods: Choose from a wide range of music genres and moods — from chill and acoustic to upbeat and energetic. Discover endless possibilities to match your emotions and creative vision.
  • Personal Music Library: Save your favorite AI-generated songs in your personal library. Revisit, listen, and refine them anytime.
  • Explore & Get Inspired: Discover new sounds and styles every day. Let AI spark your creativity and inspire your next melody.
  • Listen, Download & Share: Play your songs directly in the app, download them for offline listening, or share your creations with friends and social platforms.
  • Simple & Intuitive Design: Built for everyone — from beginners exploring music to creators who want a fresh source of inspiration.
